About the Rudra

Sakhawat Hossain Rudra is a developer, webmaster, logo designer, blogger, poet, documentary photographer, visual artist, internet activist, social worker and AI enthusiast based in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

In 2023, Rudra was influenced by Marxism-Leninism and the activities of the Bangladesh Students' Union. Later, He joined the union to fight for students' rights.

He founded Anatarjalik, a group of internet activists that supports the Bangladesh Students' Union (BSU). They work online to promote socialism and fight against propaganda.

Rudra founded Red Foto in 2024 with the aim of using photography to protest human rights abuses and terrorism.
