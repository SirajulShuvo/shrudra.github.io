S. Hossain

Sakhawat Hossain, also known as "S. Hossain," has a strong interest in AI and machine learning. He works as an ICT journalist and blogger. Currently, he is actively learning about machine learning and is enthusiastic about conducting research and developing innovative models. Sakhawat is a founding editor at Jantra Chinta, an IT magazine, where he focuses on writing about computer science and programming. Additionally, he regularly writes articles on dev.to, a popular blogging platform. Sakhawat dedicates his time as a volunteer at Bangladesh Open Source Network (BdOSN) and serves as a mentor at Scratch Bangladesh.
